Ball Don't Lie

I've been playing basketball for more than half my life and I still can't get enough of it. I can thank my stepdad for this passion as he taught me everything about the game. The sport itself even brought my family together. My mom plays, my cousins and my uncle, and we would regularly have games or just shoot around as a family. Even to this day watch basketball and am mesmerized by the athleticism of these athletes and how much fun it looks to play at an elite level.
